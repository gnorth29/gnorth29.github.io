Enterprise Security Maturity & Compliance

The Strategic Problem

Identified a critical gap in identity protection and baseline hardening, with an organizational Microsoft Secure Score at the 50th percentile. In a critical infrastructure environment, this represented an unmitigated audit liability and heightened breach probability.

The Director-Level Solution

Re-engineered the tenant security architecture by designing and orchestrating the deployment of Microsoft Intune policies to enforce "Desired State Configuration." Managed a phased rollout strategy to ensure zero operational downtime for 24/7 utility services.

Measurable Growth Increased Secure Score from 50th to 70th percentile.
Audit Readiness Streamlined NERC-CIP and insurance compliance reporting.
Risk Reduction Standardized identity governance across the enterprise fleet.
"By focusing on foundational identity governance, we moved the needle from 'average' to 'leading,' ensuring every network entry point is governed by a verifiable security standard."

Continuous Security Validation

The Strategic Problem

Traditional point-in-time vulnerability scans provided data without context. The organization lacked "exploitability intelligence"—the ability to prove which vulnerabilities posed an actual threat to mission-critical services.

The Director-Level Solution

Transitioned the enterprise from reactive scanning to a continuous automated penetration testing model. Led the selection and implementation of the NodeZero platform to proactively map internal and external attack paths.

Cost Optimization Replaced infrequent manual testing with high-fidelity, weekly automated validation.
Remediation Efficiency Focused engineering resources on proven, exploitable attack paths.
Executive Transparency Provided verifiable proof of defensive control effectiveness to leadership.
"We stopped guessing which vulnerabilities mattered and started proving which ones were exploitable, optimizing our security budget for maximum risk reduction."

Human Risk Management & Behavioral Analytics

The Strategic Problem

The organization lacked a quantitative method to measure user resilience against phishing. High click rates represented a primary vector for ransomware and credential harvesting in a high-stakes liaison environment.

The Director-Level Solution

Leveraged investigative expertise to design a data-driven Human Risk program. Engineered complex simulations and used behavioral analytics to identify high-risk departments for surgical training intervention.

Quantifiable Resilience Delivered targeted behavior improvements evidenced by decreased simulation failure rates.
Strategic Advisory Briefed executive stakeholders on human-capital risk trends as a board-level metric.
Cultural Maturity Integrated security requirements into daily business operations and idea creation.
"We treated human risk with the same analytical rigor as firewall logs, turning our workforce into a proactive first-line defensive sensor."
